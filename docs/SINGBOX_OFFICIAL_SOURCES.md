## Notes for this repo
- Do not treat libbox as a paid SDK or a separate subscription.
- Keep server/core/mobile versions close to each other.
- Android and Apple official clients are the most realistic embedding reference.
- This starter already has TUN/VPN entry points; the missing piece is the native core inside APK/IPA.
